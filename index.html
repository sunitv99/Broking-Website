FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ON HOW TO APPLY FOR AN EDUCATION LOAN

Q1. What are the Documents Required to Open a Demat Account?

To open a trading plus demat account with SVK Broking, the following documents are required:

  • Pan Card
  • Bank Proof
  • Valid residence address proof

Once the details are verified, the application gets processed and the welcome kit is dispatched to the concerned individual.

Q2. Are there any Charges for Opening a Demat Account? When is it charged?

There is no account opening fees for demat and trading account.
However, there is an Annual Maintenance charge of Rs. 450/- and a
onetime charge of Rs. 50/- for POA and Rs. 36/- for KRA along with applicable service tax.

However, there are free lifetime demat account offers. Fill in the details to know more .

Q3. What are the advantages of opening an online Demat Account?

Advantages of an online DEMAT Account are:

  • Less paperwork since securities are held in electronic form
  • Secure and convenient way to keep track of shares
  • Saves time
  • All in one - Trade in Equity, gold ETFs, and commodity futures
